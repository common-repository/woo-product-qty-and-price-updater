Woocommerce product qty and price updater makes it very convenient and fast in updating product quantity and price in woocommerce powered shop.

== Description ==

Woocommerce product qty and price updater uses ajax to process quantity and price updating in the background. Since csv export and import are both handled through ajax without saving a hard copy of the csv on the server, it is very efficient, very fast, without the need of cleaning up after a successful update.


== Installation ==


1. Upload `Woocommerce Product Qty and Price Updater` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
